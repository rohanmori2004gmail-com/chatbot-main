About Us

Welcome to GECR's innovative project on developing a college inquiry chatbot! Spearheaded by our esteemed mentor,Prof. Sheetal J. Nagar, and diligently crafted by our talented students, Rutvik Teraiya,Harsh Tank,Meet Garasiya and Rohan Mori this endeavor represents the pinnacle of our commitment to excellence and technological advancement.

Rutvik Teraiya and Harsh Tank, with their unparalleled enthusiasm and relentless pursuit of innovation, have brought this vision to life. Their collaborative efforts have resulted in the creation of a sophisticated chatbot designed to streamline the college inquiry process, providing instant assistance and accurate information to all stakeholders.

This project embodies our commitment to fostering creativity, critical thinking, and technological proficiency among our students. Through initiatives like this, we strive to prepare our graduates to thrive in an ever-evolving global landscape, equipped with the skills and knowledge necessary to make meaningful contributions to society.

Join us as we embark on this exciting journey of exploration and discovery, redefining the boundaries of higher education one innovation at a time. Welcome to the future of inquiry, welcome to chatBot.
